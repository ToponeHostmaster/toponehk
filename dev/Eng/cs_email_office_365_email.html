Email Hosting – Office 365 Email

What is Microsoft Office 365?
Microsoft Office 365 is a subscription-based online office and software plus services suite which offers access to various services and software built around the Microsoft Office platform. Our company uses Office365 to provide enterprise-grade email services. (Learn more details)

Exchange Online
Exchange Online is an email platform which delievers hosted email service with high security. Messages are kept protected by anti-malware and anti-spam filters. User can access to email, calendar, and contacts from virtually anywhere, at any time, on desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
SharePoint Online
SharePoint Online is a web application platform which enables an organization to easily create and manage custom team-focused and project-focused sites for collaboration. In addition, it’s possible to deploy an organization-wide intranet portal used to disseminate information and news across the organization.
Microsoft® Lync™ Online Intuitive communications capabilities across presence, instant messaging, audio/video calling, and a rich online meeting experience that includes PC–audio, video, and web conferencing.
